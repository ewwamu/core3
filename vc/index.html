The Vintage Collection focuses on New York City's informal sitting spaces in history. The pictures are sourced from the NYPL's Picture Collection, and many of the folders I pulled these images from were titled, "New York City - Life". I was taken by this because I found it reflected a very interesting part of our culture, in which we enjoy loitering, schmoozing, and hanging about in places that weren't made for that purpose. The pictures themselves are also incredibly captivating, many of them taken in portraiture, which I wasn't able to do.
